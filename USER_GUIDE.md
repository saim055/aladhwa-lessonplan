## ğŸ“‹ Understanding Your Lesson Plan

### DOK Levels Explained

**DOK Level 1-2** (Those Needing Assistance)
- Recall and basic understanding
- Identify, define, list, describe
- Step-by-step guided activities

**DOK Level 2-3** (Average/Middle Ability)
- Application and analysis
- Explain, compare, investigate
- Problem-solving with some independence

**DOK Level 3-4** (Upper Ability)
- Analysis, evaluation, creation
- Evaluate, justify, design
- Complex thinking and justification

**DOK Level 4** (Gifted/Talented)
- Extended thinking and synthesis
- Create, defend, synthesize
- Real-world complex applications

### V/A/K Indicators

Activities are marked with learning styles:
- **V** (Visual): Charts, graphs, diagrams, videos
- **A** (Auditory): Discussions, explanations, presentations
- **K** (Kinesthetic): Hands-on, experiments, movement

This helps you balance activities for all learners!

### Task Structure

**Cooperative Tasks (15 minutes)**
- Group work activities
- Collaborative problem-solving
- Peer discussion and teamwork

**Independent Tasks (15 minutes)**
- Individual application
- Personal demonstration of learning
- Self-paced work
